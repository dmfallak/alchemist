# Task: Procure High-Precision Hardware for EXP-001

## Objective
Acquire the necessary components to transition from basic fluid observations to high-memory pilot-wave walking.

## 🌟 PI Recommended "Path to LOG-004" (Verified Setup)
*This configuration is optimized for Linux-based lab control and high-memory stability.*

- [ ] **Signal Source:** **SMSL SU-1 USB DAC** (~$110 CAD).
    - **Specs:** AK4493S Chip, 32-bit/768kHz.
    - **Linux Status:** Verified UAC2 compliant.
- [ ] **Power:** **Fosi Audio TP-02 Subwoofer Amp** (~$90 CAD).
    - **Critical Feature:** Variable Frequency Control (30Hz–260Hz).
- [ ] **Motor:** **Dayton Audio TT25-8 Puck** (~$35 CAD).
    - *Why:* Tactile transducer for vertical displacement.
- [ ] **The Arena:** **100mm Borosilicate Glass Petri Dish** (~$20 CAD).
    - *Why:* Flat, rigid, and chemically inert for high-purity IPA cleaning.
- [ ] **Mounting:** **3M VHB Double-Sided Tape** (~$15 CAD).
    - *Why:* Securely bonds glass to the motor while absorbing micro-hiss.

## I. Essential Consumables
- [ ] **Fluid:** **Team Associated RC Shock Oil** (~$20 CAD). (200cSt bath, 500cSt droplets).
- [ ] **Needles:** **Btektech 27G Blunt Tips** (~$15 CAD).
- [ ] **Syringes:** 1ml Luer Lock Syringes.
- [ ] **Cleaner:** 99.9% Isopropyl Alcohol (IPA) for surface maintenance.

## II. DIY Isolation Strategy (Zero-Cost Pro Tier)
- [ ] **The Base:** Concrete paving stone or granite slab.
- [ ] **Damping:** Folded yoga mat or high-density foam (decouple from lab bench).

## III. Verification
- [ ] Verify Luer Lock compatibility between needles and syringes.
- [ ] Confirm amplifier wattage matches the transducer's impedance (8 ohms for TT25-8).
- [ ] **Linux Check:** Run `aplay -l` to confirm the SMSL SU-1 is recognized by the kernel.
