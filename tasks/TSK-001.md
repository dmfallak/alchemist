# Task: Draft Detailed Procedure for EXP-001

## Objective
Establish a high-precision, repeatable protocol for sustaining a walking silicone oil droplet in the 50Hz–80Hz vibration regime.

## I. Environmental Pre-flight
- [ ] **Thermal Equilibrium:** Ambient temperature must be $22^\circ\text{C} \pm 1^\circ\text{C}$. Oil must sit for 2 hours in-situ.
- [ ] **Air Isolation:** Place the "Stellarator Shield" (acrylic lid) over the bath.
- [ ] **Vibration Dampening:** Ensure the transducer is on a heavy granite/concrete base.
- [ ] **Surface Purity:** Triple-rinse the glass petri dish with 99.9% IPA.

## II. The Bath Setup
- [ ] **Fluid Depth:** Fill the 100mm petri dish to a depth of **5.0mm – 6.0mm**.
- [ ] **Leveling:** Use a spirit level to ensure the bath is level to within 0.1°. Tilt will cause droplets to drift "downhill."

## III. Mechanical Stability Check (The Laser Test)
- [ ] **Setup:** Strike the surface with a laser at 45°.
- [ ] **Pass:** Reflected dot stretches into a clean, vertical line.
- [ ] **Fail:** Dot forms an "8" or circle (indicates rocking).

## IV. Hardware Calibration (LOG-003)
- [ ] **Faraday Threshold ($\gamma_F$):** Increase amplitude at 80Hz until the surface ripples. Record this setting.
- [ ] **Walking Regime:** Set gain to ~90% of $\gamma_F$.

## V. Droplet Deployment & Observation
- [ ] **Technique:** Gently deposit $0.8\text{--}1.0\,\text{mm}$ droplet using 27G blunt needle.
- [ ] **Observation:** Use **Shadowgraphy** (point-light from above) to see the horseshoe-shaped pilot wave.

## VI. Manual "Sleep Phase" Transition
- [ ] Record all numerical data (Hz, Temp, Threshold Voltage).
- [ ] Update `STRATEGY.md` and `data/bridge.json` before ending session.
